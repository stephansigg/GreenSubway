% Abstract
\begin{abstract}
Underground transportation systems have significant impacts on energy consumption. While trains has been observed regarding energy efficiency, the subsystems of metro stations and surroundings, such as ventilation, escalator and lightning are mostly unexplored.
For energy optimal control of these subsystem the SEAM4US project developed a predictive control architecture. The control architecture proactively perform energy management tasks based on situations taking place in the future. The passenger model provides prediction about the count of persons. In this work we analyse the CCTV data which are basis of the passenger density prediction.
\end{abstract}
